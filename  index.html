Fitness Recipe Mobile App Design

Recifit is a mobile app in concept that encompasses both fitness and healthy eating, with nutritional needs being the main component. There are not many apps in the market that take a user’s BMI into consideration, while also providing recipes that cater to an individual’s needs and preferences. With this in mind, we want to create a service to provide personalized, healthy recipes that help meet the user’s weight goals and lifestyle.

Non-Profit Website Redesign

World Parks Inc is a non-profit organization aiming to achieve the preservation and the expansion of global world parks. Its current website is in desperate need of an update. The plethora of information within the site is not organized intuitively and there are pages dedicated to information that could easily be consolidated with other pages with relevant information. Additionally the UI design is also outdated which causes the organization to appear unprofessional to the users, undermining its mission, which is spreading awareness and fundraising for global environmental conservation.
